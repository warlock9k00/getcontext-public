Support

Get Context is built and operated by one person. There is no support desk, no ticket system, and no chatbot. You write to me, I write back.

Email

hello@context.select

Response time: within 5 business days, usually faster.

What I can help with

  • The bot didn't join a meeting, or joined and left.
  • Notes are missing or look broken.
  • You want your data deleted.
  • You can't uninstall the app or revoke access.
  • You found a bug.
  • You have a feature request.

What helps me help you faster

  • The Zoom meeting ID (or join URL) of the affected meeting.
  • Approximate time the meeting happened.
  • What you expected to happen vs. what actually happened.
  • Screenshots, if relevant.

Privacy / data requests

To request a copy or deletion of your data, write to the same address with subject "Privacy request". I respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.
