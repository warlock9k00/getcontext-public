Meeting notes that stay with you.

Get Context joins your Zoom meetings, transcribes them with Groq Whisper, and turns each call into a searchable note in your private knowledge base. Built for people who run a lot of meetings and remember none of them.

What it does

  • Joins any Zoom meeting you host or are invited to (after Marketplace approval).
  • Records audio, transcribes with Groq Whisper Large v3.
  • Generates a TL;DR, action items, decisions, and open questions using Claude.
  • Writes the result into your private GitHub repo as Markdown — yours forever.
  • Sends you a Telegram notification when notes are ready.

What it doesn't do

  • Sell or share your meeting data with anyone.
  • Train models on your conversations.
  • Keep audio after transcription. The recording is deleted once notes are written.
  • Touch your existing files without explicit permission.
